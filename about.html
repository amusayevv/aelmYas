ABOUT
I’m Yasmina Aarab, a CREATIVE DESIGNER based in Catalonia, Spain, with an unstoppable passion for ART & DESIGN. If you are wondering where aelmyas comes from, they are the initials of my full name.
My journey is a constant cycle of EXPLORATION and LEARNING, always pushing beyond limits. Alongside my digital graphic design studies, I’ve been diving into the vibrant world of poster design for about a year. Art has been my driving force since childhood, and now, as I’m about to delve deeper and I couldn’t be energized for more.
As a student of Digital and Interactive Design, my goal is to expand my skills and knowledge: from crafting captivating visuals to exploring the latest trends. I’m always in motion, seeking out new inspirations.
